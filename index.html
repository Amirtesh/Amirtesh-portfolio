About Me

I am a Biotechnology student at Vellore Institute of Technology with a strong focus on computational biology, bioinformatics, and drug discovery. My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, molecular modeling, structural bioinformatics, and genomics.


Currently, I'm collaborating with researchers on projects involving natural inhibitors for diabetes, glaucoma, and triple negative breast cancer treatment using computational methods such as molecular docking, molecular dynamics simulations, and binding free energy calculations.

I've developed several tools to streamline research workflows, including Torchify (a PyTorch library with 2,500+ downloads) and Automated-Virtual-Screening. My expertise spans from next-generation sequencing data analysis to complete computational drug discovery pipelines.


I'm pursuing my B.Tech in Biotechnology at VIT Vellore (2023-2027) with a CGPA of 9.01/10 and actively seeking opportunities to apply my computational skills to solve complex biological problems.
